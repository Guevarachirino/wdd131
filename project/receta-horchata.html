Horchata Recipe



Ingredients


  • 1 cup of long-grain white rice
  • 5 cups of water
  • 1 cup of milk (optional, for creaminess)
  • 1/2 cup of sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon (plus more for serving)


Instructions:

Rinse the Rice: Rinse the rice under cold water to remove excess starch. Drain well.


Soak the Rice: In a bowl, combine the rinsed rice and 2 cups of water. Let it soak for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight.


Blend: After soaking, transfer the rice and water to a blender. Blend until the rice is coarsely ground.


Strain: Pour the mixture through a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth into a large pitcher. Press the mixture to extract as much liquid as possible. Discard the solids.


Add Ingredients: To the strained liquid, add the remaining 3 cups of water, milk (if using), sugar, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and a pinch of salt. Stir well to combine.


Chill: Refrigerate the horchata for at least an hour to let the flavors meld.


Serve: Serve over ice and sprinkle with additional ground cinnamon if desired. Enjoy!
