My favorite dessert

Rice Pudding



Ingredients


  • 1 Cup rice
  • 3 Cups water
  • 3 Cups organic whole milk
  • Cinnamon
  • 1 Can Evaporated milk
  • 1 Condensed milk
  • Salt

Directions

Combine water, cinnamon and pinch of salt in a large sauce pan and bring to a boil. After that add reduce the heat to low and add the rice and organic whole milk, evaporated milk and condensed milk. Once the rice got a soft and creamy consistency (about 20 to 25 minutes), remove it from the heat. Our rice pudding is ready!! You can enjoy It warm or cold add a little cinnamon to decorate.
