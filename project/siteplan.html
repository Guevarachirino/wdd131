Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to share some of my favorite foods from my country with my friends who don't speak Spanish. I also want to share a piece of my culture with everyone.

Scenarios

The Salvadoran Cuisine audience is people who are passionate about the culinary culture of El Salvador.

The age demographic varies from 20 to 60 years old.

Potential followers can access this website from any device, such as a mobile phone, a tablet, or computer.
